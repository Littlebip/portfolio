PhotoBuddies

___ PhotoBuddies is a Rails app prototype that helps photographers get in touch with their local photography community and share their art.
___ I designed, developed, pushed to production and demoed this product in two weeks as a team of four junior developers as a final project during my Web Development Bootcamp at Le Wagon. This project taught me a lot in terms of teamwork, communication skills and organization.
___ It was very exciting to work on this project from A to Z, and being able to deliver after only two weeks made our team very proud.
___ After the end of the bootcamp, I have kept working on the project, namely getting rid of n + 1 relations, adding some testing and refactoring the code to make it cleaner.

OnMangeKoi

___ I am very proud to present my latest project which is still ...cooking 🔥🔥🔥It is a Rails web app that helps people organizing their meals.
___ This project started from a very personal pain: I love to cook, but designing meal plans can take a lot of time: making sure that each meal is varied, nutritious and cooked with seasonal ingredients... but also fits the likings and allergies of the people you live with can be a pain in the back.
___ So this is what OnMangeKoi (in French: What are we eating?) does for you: it designs your meal plans according to your criteria, but also provides you with the corresponding recipes AND also makes your shopping list for you! The web app is still in design right now, and I will be very excited to see it go live and use it myself!
___ As my French-only mom asked for a version she could use, I am planning to create two versions: one in English using a RESTful API for recipes and ingredients, and another one in French using my mom's personal stack of recipes 😁
___ Both versions should make me practice a lot of varied developing skills.
